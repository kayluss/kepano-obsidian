## Drinks
	Sizes Soda - Lg 32, Md 22, Sm 16 
	
	 Iced coffee
		L = 6 pumps of syrup
		M = 5 Pumps of syrup
		S = 4 pumps of syrup
		Process
			To make an iced coffee at McDonald's, employees 
			combine ice, a liquid sugar or flavored syrup, light cream, and their brewed premium roast coffee in a cup==. 
	Ingredients
	- **Ice:** Fills the cup up to a specific line.
	- **Syrup:** A specific number of pumps of flavored syrup (vanilla, sugar-free vanilla, or caramel) or unflavored liquid sugar, depending on the size of the drink.
	- **Cream:** A set amount of light cream is added, often automated by the machine. In Canada, you can also opt for 2% milk.
	- **Brewed Coffee:** McDonald's uses a strong, proprietary blend of 100% Arabica bean coffee, brewed fresh. 
	Steps for Preparation
	1. **Add ice:** The cup is filled with ice to the designated fill line.
	2. **Add flavor and cream:** The correct number of pumps of desired syrup or liquid sugar and the required amount of cream are dispensed into the cup.
	3. **Pour coffee:** Freshly brewed, strong coffee is added to the mixture.
	4. **Stir:** The drink is stirred (around 12 times for a medium) to combine all the ingredients thoroughly.
