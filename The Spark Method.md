**[[Notetaking]]** is overrated. 
It turns the mind into a passive, info-hoarding machine.
**Notemaking** is the cure.
It turns the mind into a thriving, sensemaking system.
And the **Spark Method** is the simplest, most fundamental form of notemaking.
![[IMG_3447.png]]

---
This method is very similar to photography methods that ask what you notice, how you compose it, and can it be grouped with other phots you took to create a project. Projects in [[Photography]] usually end up as a zine or book.
